Track Your Body

This project appears to be a simple browser game where the player controls a character on the screen using movements of the right hand.

The rules of the game are as follows:

  • Control: Use your right hand to control the position of the character on the screen.
  • Goal: Avoid getting hit by the passing cars by moving up or down.
  • Lives: You have 5 lives. The game ends when all your lives are lost.
  • Survival: Try to survive for as long as you can by avoiding the cars.

Device Motion

This minigame utilizes the gyroscope in your device to move a spaceship in space.

The rules of the game are as follows:

  • Control: Use your mobile phone or devtool on your computer to move the spaceship.
  • Goal: Avoid getting hit by gray asteroids by moving around.
  • Lives: You have 5 lives. The game ends when all your lives are lost.
  • Survival: Try to collect as many yellow stars as possible.
